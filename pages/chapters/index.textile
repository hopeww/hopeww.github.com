<section id="op">

		<tr>
			<td><img src="{{ ASSET_PATH }}/twitter/images/chapters/about/board.jpg" alt=""></td>
			<td>
				<h2>Locally Operated</h2>
				Each chapter is operated by a locally appointed leader and a board of advisors.
				<p>Within the local chapter, these servant leaders, are chosen because they are deemed adequately equipped and having a passion for serving the poor through the programs of HOPE <i>worldwide</i> of Canada.  
				<p>The board of advisors meets quarterly to direct the chapter leader on how best to implement HOPE <i>worldwide</i> in the church and in the community.	
				<p>Most importantly, they also serve as the voice of the volunteers.
			</td>
		</tr>
		<tr>
			<td>
				Local chapters are responsible for local program development, human resources, financial and fundraising decisions.

				<p>Moreover, each chapter is responsible for local volunteer recruitment and management.  They are also responsible to directing all the volunteer run programs, and organization with the goal of meeting the needs of that community.
			</td>
			<td><img src="{{ ASSET_PATH }}/twitter/images/chapters/about/programs.jpg" alt=""></td>
		</tr>
