<h2><a name="vision">Vision & Mission</a></h2>

h4. Vision

HOPE <i>worldwide</i>'s vision is to bring hope and change to the world's neediest people.

h4. Mission 

HOPE _worldwide_ is an international charity that changes lives by harnessing the compassion and commitment of dedicated staff and volunteers to deliver sustainable, high-impact, community-based services to the poor and needy.
